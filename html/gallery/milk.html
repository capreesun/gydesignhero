Brand Design â€“ 2021


Zhao Ri Wei Pin is a very 'SLOW' animal husbandry company. When establishing the farm, they found the soil poor, so they spent several years improving the soil fertility, eventually raising good cows and producing good milk, and using circular farming to produce more vegetables and fruits of the highest quality. This company's dedication to quality, integrity and reverence for nature is rare and valuable in the "fast" times - and the brand we built for Zhao Ri Wei Pin was designed to emphasize this spirit.
