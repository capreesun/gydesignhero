Guang Yu

Designer, Creative Director


Guang Yu is graphic designer and creative director at A Black Cover Design Studio redefining what it means to design in an age that prioritizes unique perspectives and new approaches. Heavily utilitarian yet experimental, his ethos is defined not by his persona, but by the diversity and flexibility of the work itself.





He is most known for his work with A Black Cover Design, a creative branding agency based in Beijing that Guang Yu co-founded with Nod Young, another Chinese graphic designer in 2015. With ABCD, Guang Yu has worked on dozens of projects and fundementally changed the landscape of design in China, creating delightful and inventive artifacts that enrich the everyday lives of Chinese people.
